Service Level Agreement

Airlines operate around the clock. Here's what we commit to for uptime and support response — applicable to paid plans.

Uptime commitment

We target 99.9% uptime (up to 8.7 hours of downtime per year), measured on a calendar-month basis and excluding planned maintenance.

Planned maintenance

We notify account administrators at least 72 hours in advance of planned maintenance and schedule it during lowest-traffic hours for your station where possible.

Service credits

If we fall below the uptime commitment in a given month, eligible accounts receive a 10% service credit for every 0.1% below target, capped at 30% of that month's fees.

Exclusions: force majeure events, issues caused by customer misconfiguration, and outages caused by third-party dependencies outside our control.
