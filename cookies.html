Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies AeroCater uses and how to manage your preferences. You can update your choices at any time using the "Manage cookie preferences" link in the footer.

1. What cookies we use

TypePurposeCan be disabled?
EssentialSession management, authentication, securityNo — required for the Service to function
FunctionalRemembering language and display preferencesYes
AnalyticsUnderstanding aggregate usage patterns to improve the ServiceYes

We do not use marketing or advertising cookies.

2. Your choices

When you first visit aerocater.ca, you'll see a banner asking you to accept or customize cookie categories. Essential cookies are always active since the Service cannot function without them. You can change your choices at any time via "Manage cookie preferences," available in the footer on every page.

3. Consent records

We log a record of your consent choice (timestamp and category selections) to maintain an audit trail, as required under GDPR.

4. Contact

Questions about cookies: privacy@aerocater.ca
