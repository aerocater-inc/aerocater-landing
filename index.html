Why AeroCater exists

Eight years of running catering ops taught us exactly where the old systems break.

Everything dispatch actually needs

Not a repurposed trucking platform. Every feature is built around the specific mechanics of in-flight catering.

01 — AUTO-DISPATCH

Assignments that respect real constraints

Matches trucks and drivers by drive-transition time, workload balance, and turnaround risk — and never books a driver outside their shift or double-assigns a truck.

02 — TROLLEY TRACKING

Chain-of-custody, not guesswork

Every trolley carries its own QR identity. Know exactly when it left the kitchen, boarded the aircraft, and came back — with a timestamped scan history.

03 — DRIVER MOBILE APP

Built for a phone in a high-vis vest

Drivers check in and scan trolleys from a personal link — no separate login, no desktop required, works one-handed on the ramp.

04 — FLEET HEALTH

See a problem before it's a delay

Live hydraulic cycles, service windows, and alerts surface issues before a truck fails mid-turnaround.

Built to be trusted with live ops

Straight answers about how AeroCater is built and run — no vague reassurances.

Encrypted, access-controlled data

All data is encrypted in transit and stored with row-level security, so every user only ever sees the station and role they're authorized for. Hosted on Supabase — SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure.

Individual driver identity

No shared logins. Every scan and every check-in is tied to a specific person, with a full audit trail.

Built around real operational timing

Food-safety windows, turnaround buffers, and shift boundaries are enforced in the dispatch logic itself, not left to a spreadsheet.
